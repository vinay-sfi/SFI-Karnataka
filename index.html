Our Mission
Fighting for student rights in Karnataka

SFI Karnataka has been at the forefront of every major student struggle in the state — from campus fee hike protests to fights for hostel facilities and democratic student union elections.

We stand with students across government colleges, aided institutions, and universities, ensuring their voices are heard at every level of governance.

Education for All

Demanding free, quality public education accessible to every student regardless of economic background.

Campus Democracy

Ensuring democratic student union elections across all colleges and universities in Karnataka.

Social Justice

Fighting against caste discrimination, communalism, and all forms of inequality in educational spaces.

Women's Safety

Championing gender equality and safety for women students in every institution across the state.

About SFI Karnataka
Who We Are

The Student Federation of India (SFI) was founded in 1970 as a progressive student organisation affiliated with the Communist Party of India (Marxist). The Karnataka unit has been active since the organisation's early years, building a strong base across the state's colleges and universities.

SFI Karnataka operates through a network of district and taluk committees, running student union elections across thousands of colleges. The organisation is particularly active in Bengaluru, Mysuru, Mangaluru, Shivamogga, and Dharwad districts.

Central to SFI's ideology is the belief that education is a right, not a commodity. The organisation actively opposes the commercialisation and privatisation of education, fighting instead for a democratically controlled, inclusive public education system.

Over the decades, SFI Karnataka has produced leaders who have gone on to serve in public life, academia, and social movements — carrying forward the values of progressive student politics.

State Committee

The Karnataka State Committee coordinates all district units and represents the state at the national level.
